About Us

Seti Gandaki Prashodhan Private Limited is Nepal’s leading supplier of high-strength concrete aggregates, sourced from the Seti Gandaki River’s Himalayan quartz and basalt deposits. Established in 2015, we operate a state-of-the-art facility in Chauthe, Pokhara, delivering broken stone, gravel, sand, and crushed fines for construction and infrastructure.

Our sustainable practices include 85% water recycling and adherence to Nepal’s environmental standards, ensuring quality and reliability.
